Welcome to Olorunda Baptist Church

Olorunda Baptist Church is a group of loving christian families. We're together with God because of his kindness and goodness to humanity. Together as a family, we're learning to embody this kindness and commitment to one another and beyond into the wider world. And all this is possible because of Jesus, the one who brings us together and keeps us together.
